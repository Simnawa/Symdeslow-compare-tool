How to Use

  1. Make a copy of Symdeslow Compare Tool through the link received.
  2. Paste your data in Sheet 1 and Sheet 2.
  3. Key-based comparison mode: Set your comparison key and data range for comparison. Direct column-to-column comparison mode: skip this step.
  4. Click the "Compare Now" button to generate highlights and reports.

Use Cases & FAQ

  • What types of users is this tool designed for? Professionals working with Google Sheets who need fast and accurate data comparison across departments. Suitable for teams handling financial data, operations, auditing, quality control, or any repetitive dataset review.
  • Can it handle very large datasets? The tool works well for most datasets, but for very large sheets (processing time over ~6 minutes), it's recommended to split the data into smaller sections for smoother performance.
  • Do I need any external software? No, Symdeslow Compare Tool works entirely within Google Sheets.
  • What types of comparison modes are available? Direct column-to-column comparison and key-based comparison, both with four-color highlighting for easy visual tracking.
